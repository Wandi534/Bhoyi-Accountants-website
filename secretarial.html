Secretarial Services

Our secretarial services ensure that your company remains legally compliant and structurally sound. We provide expert advice on the appropriate legal structure for your business and handle everything from company registration to reactivating deregistered entities. We also support Public Benefit Organizations with full Section 18A compliance and approval.

  • Company registrations via CIPC
  • Advice on legal structure (PTY LTD, Trust, NPO, etc.)
  • Annual return submissions to CIPC
  • CIPC reactivation and backdated return filings
  • Assistance with PBO compliance and SARS registration
  • Preparation of financial statements for PBOs
  • Section 18A application for tax-deductible donations
