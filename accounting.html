Accounting Services

Bhoyi Accountants offers expert accounting and tax services tailored to meet legal and financial obligations across all client types. Our accounting work is guided by the International Financial Reporting Standards for Small to Medium Enterprises (IFRS for SMEs) and ensures full compliance with SARS and the Companies Act.

  • Company and individual tax returns (including provisional tax)
  • Capital Gains Tax (CGT)
  • Payroll taxes
  • Annual Financial Statements preparation, review, and compilation (IFRS for SMEs)
  • Tax planning and reduction strategies
  • VAT registration and submissions (monthly, biannual, or annual)
  • Applications for remission of interest and penalties
  • Overdue tax and VAT return submissions (up to 15 years)
  • SARS e-filing profile transfers and compliance checks
